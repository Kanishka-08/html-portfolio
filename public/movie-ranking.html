The Best Movies According to Kanishka

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Taare Zameen Par

This movie illucidates the idea of being lost .
It's okay to be lost and finding your own way in this world of contradictions and traditions .

Wake up Sid

Although its a flop movie ,it very well illustrates the idea of finding your hidden talents and passsions and become independent in this world of harmonal hijack.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

Although its an old movie ,it enhances the importance of enjoying life to the fullest while working hard through the life of a successful yet unhappy businessman.
